Frequently Asked Questions

How long does generation take? ▼

Depending on document length and complexity, generation takes between 1 to 5 minutes. You'll see real-time progress updates throughout the process.

What formats can I download? ▼

You can download your documents as PDF, DOCX (Microsoft Word), and PPTX (PowerPoint) files. All exports maintain professional formatting.

Is my data secure? ▼

Yes. All AI processing happens through secure backend proxies. No API keys or sensitive data are ever exposed to the browser. We use a multi-model safety pipeline to ensure content quality.

Can I edit documents after generation? ▼

Absolutely. The built-in editor lets you modify any section, adjust formatting, and preview changes in real-time before exporting.
